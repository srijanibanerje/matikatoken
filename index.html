Ecosystem

A Unified Web3 Universe

Four interconnected pillars powered by MTIK, designed to deliver real utility across the decentralized economy.

MATIKAPAY

Users can hold ,received and send crypto currency on MATIKA PAY. It is a contactless, borderless and secure cryptocurrency payment technology designed on MATIKA ECOSYSTEM. Shop with crypto or send crypto to friends and family worldwide. This is the most secure payment system with low transaction fee.

MATIKA NFT MARKETPLACE

Non-Fungible Token or NFT marketplace is a marketplace that functions as a public Blockchain platform. However, this platform is gaining traction and driving developers and businesses to construct a marketplace, despite being in its nascent stage. Online marketplaces for digital assets are the recent talk of the fintech town. It is a platform that makes it simple to store and sell NFTs. These tokens are generally available for purchase or auction at a set price. Users have to create an account, upload digital artwork, and sell their work on the MATIKA NFT marketplace.

MATIKASWAP

Matika swap is a Decentralised Exchange where users can trade their favourite crypto assets with low fees, also provide liquidity on Matika liquidity pool. MatikaSwap is a decentralized exchange built on the Polygon Network. It enables its users to swap their tokens easily. The original idea of DeFi and DEX was to decentralized world finances.

MATIKAVERSE

It's a digital environment complete with augmented reality and virtual reality technologies that will change how we connect, communicate, and access information. We all are known that metaverse is the future of online virtual reality and Matikaverse will play the biggest role on meta verse utility.
